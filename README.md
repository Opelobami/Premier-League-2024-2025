## Project Overview  

This project presents an in-depth **data analytics study** of the **Premier League 2024/2025 season**, focusing on **team performance**, **player efficiency**, and **salary structure** using **Microsoft Excel**.  
The goal is to translate complex data into **actionable insights** that help decision-makers such as club executives, analysts, scouts and even recruiters make informed strategic choices.

The analysis is structured across four dashboards:  

1. **Overview Dashboard** - Summary of matches, results, goals, and standings.  
2. **Salary Dashboard** - Evaluation of player and team wage distribution and efficiency.  
3. **Player Statistics Dashboard** - Deep dive into player metrics and performance.  
4. **Team Statistics Dashboard** - Assessment of team performance, discipline, and possession dominance.  

---

## Why This Project Matters  

The Premier League is a high-stakes environment where **every decision carries financial and tactical weight**.  
This analysis matters because it:  

- Highlights **salary-performance imbalances** that affect club profitability.  
- Reveals **discipline and tactical trends** that directly influence match outcomes.

Without this kind of insight, clubs risk **overspending, underperforming, and missing competitive opportunities** in the transfer and tactical market.
